About Us


Masters Collection is over Two Decades old Indian Art Gallery based in Kolkata, India. The primary vision of the gallery is to procure, advice & promote an exclusive range of Indian Contemporary Art, Indian Modern Art & Indian Paintings by exploring and evaluating artists from across India.Over last few years Indian Art has gained fame not only at national but international level and it is with this aim that the gallery currently showcases some of the finest collection of Indian Artworks & Indian Paintings of eminent and famous artists of Bengal & rest of India.
